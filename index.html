Ultimate Chicken Horse

Screw with your Friends!

Run through the level, build as you play, and place deadly traps as you try to reach the goal in this chaotic party platformer. If you can make it but your friends can't, you score points!




Place sawblades, flamethrowers, crossbows, barbed wire, and more in this no-holds-barred battle for ultimate victory!

Available now on Nintendo Switch, PlayStation, Xbox, macOS, Microsoft Windows, and Linux.
